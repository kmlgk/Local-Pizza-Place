Family recipes, wood-fired since 2011

What started as a single oven in a strip mall has grown into Riverside's favorite neighborhood pizzeria — same dough recipe, same family, same oven that never cools down.

Our story

From one oven to a Riverside institution

Forno & Co. started in 2011 when Marco Belli's grandmother's dough recipe outgrew her home kitchen. With one wood-fired oven and a folding table for four, the goal was simple: real ingredients, no shortcuts.

Fifteen years later, we're still hand-stretching every ball of dough to order, still simmering our sauce for six hours, and still delivering hot within 30 minutes — because that promise is what built this place.

Our Mission

Serve honest, wood-fired pizza made from real ingredients, delivered fast enough that it's still hot when it reaches your table.

Our Vision

To be the pizza place every neighborhood deserves — family-run, community-first, and never further than a 30-minute delivery away.

Our history

Fifteen years, one oven at a time

  1. 2011

    The first oven

    Marco and his grandmother open a four-table pizzeria on Sunset Ave with one wood-fired oven.

  2. 2015

    Delivery launches

    We added our first delivery drivers and the 30-minute promise was born.

  3. 2019

    Catering & parties

    Started catering office lunches and birthday parties across Riverside.

  4. 2026

    Online ordering & live tracking

    Launched our online ordering platform with live delivery tracking, favorites and coupons.
